Meet Craig Davidson

From Saturday boy to award-winning butcher, now leading the next chapter of the Braemar shop.

Craig Davidson started young in the shop, first helping on Saturdays before moving into butchery full time. In 2024 he was named Scottish Craft Butchers SVQ Level 2 Modern Apprentice of the Year after standing out for his knife skills, product knowledge and customer service.

That journey matters here. It speaks to practical skill, long-term commitment to the trade, and a real link to the shop customers already know. With Craig now carrying the business forward after Neil Menzies, the site should reflect that continuity as well as the new ownership.
